How to choose your cake

To ensure your cake is perfect for your special occasion, please consider the following when placing your order:

1. Begin by choosing your cake size

Which one would you prefer?

  • Mini – 1 filling layer (serves up to 6) €25
  • Small – 2 filling layers (serves 10–12) €35
  • Medium – 3 filling layers (serves 15–20) €55
  • Large – 4 filling layers (serves 25–30) €65
  • Extra Large – 25cm cake (serves 35–40) €90

2. Now, choose your sponge

Which would you like?

  • Vanilla (classic sponge)
  • Chocolate (smooth yet rich)

3. Hmm… now for the best part: the filling!

You may choose up to two delicious fillings.

Brigadeiro Bases

Nutella or specialty chocolates: additional charge may apply.

4. Finally… the finishing touch: decoration

How our cake decoration works

Finishing

Each cake is decorated with care to match the chosen flavor and the occasion. Our decorations are thoughtfully designed to create a beautiful and balanced presentation.

Please note that fruit compotes or extra sweets may involve an additional cost. This can be discussed when confirming your order.

And don’t forget to tell us your preferred ribbon color, so your cake can be even more personalized.
